Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil water and add salt
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8–10 minutes
  3. Heat oil and add garlic
  4. Add tomato sauce
  5. Mix pasta with sauce

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
